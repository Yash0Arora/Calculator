Operator	Name		            Example	

+	Addition		                5 + 2 =7	
-	Subtraction	                    5 - 2 = 3	
*	Multiplication	                5 * 2 =10	
/	Division		                5 / 2 = 2.5 |or| 18/2 = 9.0
%	Remainder	                    5 % 2 = 1 |or| 18/2 = 0	
**	Exponentiation(Gives power)	    5 ** 2 = 25      {[base]**[power]}
//	Floor division(Gives Quotient)	5 // 2 = 2 |or|  18/2 = 9
